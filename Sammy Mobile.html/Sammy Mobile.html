Arc Shot

A shot in which the subject is photographed by
an encircling or moving camera

Interlude

A brief , intervening film or sequence, not
specifically tied to the plot, that appears within a film

Prologue

A speech , preface,introduction,or brief scene preseding
the main action or plot of a film; contast to epilogue
